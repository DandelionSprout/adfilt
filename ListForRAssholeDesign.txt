! To remove obstructing general ads on phones, I recommend using Firefox for Android with the uBlock Origin extension, with various ad-related lists turned on (e.g. EasyList, AdGuard Base, AdGuard Mobile). This list will therefore not take care of such ads, as it'd be superfluous to do so.
! To remove requests to turn off adblockers on both desktops and phones, download Nano or uBO, and also use such lists as e.g. Adblock Warning Removal List, uBlock Filters, and/or Nano Defender Integration.
